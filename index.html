Cube Satellite Design

My team and I represented NUS at the Singapore Space Challenge and won a Merit award. We designed a satellite capable of co-operative docking and linking up of multiple cubesats in orbit. Our design was considered interesting because we were able to suggest a viable approach in using supercapacitors and Li-Ion batteries together efficiently. I designed the sub-system (Energy Power System) in this project.

Systems, Satellite Design, Energy, 3D Modelling
